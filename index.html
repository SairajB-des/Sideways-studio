What it is

One plan, from the idea in your head
to the final cut.

Any time an idea strikes — on a walk, mid-conversation, at 2am — tap a widget, talk it out, and Sideways transcribes it, understands it, and turns it into a structured, shoot-ready first draft automatically.

That plan then follows you through your entire workflow: a block system that organizes your script, shots, music, sound effects, and text, all in one place. Use it to run through your shot list on the shoot, open the timeline view when you’re editing. No more using five different tools.

And the more you create with it, the more it learns — your style, your shots, your rhythm — so every next video starts a little further ahead.
